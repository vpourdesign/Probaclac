How it works

Probiotics fight Candida
at every stage

A 3-step natural defence mechanism that works alongside — or instead of — antifungal medications.

1
Restore the acidic environment
Lactobacillus strains produce lactic acid, lowering vaginal pH below 4.5 — the threshold above which Candida albicans thrives.
2
Compete for adhesion sites
Probiotic Lactobacilli colonize vaginal epithelial cells, physically blocking Candida from attaching and forming biofilms.
3
Produce natural antifungal compounds
Hydrogen peroxide and bacteriocins secreted by Lactobacillus strains directly inhibit Candida albicans growth and colony formation.
4
Modulate immune response
Probiotics strengthen local mucosal immunity, reducing the inflammatory response associated with yeast infection symptoms.
